Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the app, we store the expense records you create — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what you explicitly enter into Spendly.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and personalising your experience. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

You may delete your account at any time. Upon deletion, all your personal data and expense records will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces. This involves a request to Google's servers, which may log your IP address per Google's own privacy policy. No other third-party services receive your data.

We do not integrate with analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social media trackers.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app. We aim to respond within 5 business days.
